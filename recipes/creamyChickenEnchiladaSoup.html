Creamy Chicken Enchilada Soup

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 medium white onion, halved and sliced
  • 1 clove garlic, thinly sliced
  • 1 tablespoon ancho chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 2 teaspoons brown sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt, plus more to taste
  • 2 (28-ounce) cans whole, peeled tomatoes with juice, preferably fire roasted
  • 4 ounces (about 1 heaping cup) Monterey jack cheese, shredded
  • 1 cup chicken stock
  • 11/2 cup heavy cream
  • 3 cups cooked chicken, shredded
  • 3 cups corn kernels, fresh or frozen (no need to defrost)
  • 1 (14.5-ounce) can black beans, rinsed and drained
  • 1/4 cup sour cream
  • 1/4 cup heavy cream

Wondering how to make it? Here's how.

  1. Cook the onions and spices:
    • In a large pot over medium heat, heat the oil.
    • Add the onion and cook for 5 to 7 minutes, stirring often, until soft and golden.
    • in the garlic, chili powder, oregano, brown sugar, and salt. Cook, stirring often, for 1 minute.
  2. Add the tomatoes and the cheese:
    • Stir in the tomatoes and bring the mixture to a simmer. Break up the tomatoes.
    • Simmer uncovered for 10 minutes. Stir in the shredded cheese.
  3. Blend the soup until smooth:
    • Carefully, ladle the soup into a blender.
    • Gradually increase the speed to medium-high and blend for another minute, until the soup is smooth.
  4. Add the stock and the cream:
    • Return the soup to the pot and set it over medium heat. Stir in the stock, cream, chicken, corn, and beans.
    • Bring to a gentle simmer, stirring often.
  5. Make the crema:
    • In a small bowl, stir the sour cream and milk or cream together until smooth.
    • The consistency should be pourable, like thick cream.
  6. Garnish and serve:
    • Ladle the soup into bowls and spoon some homemade crema over each.
    • Lay out the garnishes on the table in small individual bowls and let everyone top their soup to their liking.
